About Me

I’m many things; software developer, board game geek, sugar junkie. At the end of the day I’m just someone who loves to learn and discover new things. Whether it’s finding out how certain software works or exploring a new language, I’m driven to illuminate the unknown, find new perspectives and, whenever possible, share that journey with others.

This blog is intended to be as much about documenting the things I learn as it is about reaching out to like minded people who can’t/won’t stop till their curiosity is satisfied!
